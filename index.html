Meet Leora

Growing up in a family of developers and agents, Leora has been immersed in the real estate ecosystem all her life. After graduating from Chapman’s School of Communication, she started her career as a financial manager to hone her technical skills while continuing to build key relationships in the LA / OC area. Once she developed the quantitative skills to compliment her love for working with clients, she was compelled to follow her passion in real estate. Within three months as an agent, she prepped, listed, and sold two listings over asking and has three additional properties currently in escrow.

Due to Leora’s upbringing and networking, she is well connected in Southern California. Her vast knowledge of real estate development, management, and investment has led her to specialize in development opportunities and single / multi-family residences. With her experience in the fields of client services, sales, and marketing, Leora has an aptitude for organization and is a highly competent negotiator. Leora is passionate about providing exceptional customer experiences and exceeding her clients’ expectations.
